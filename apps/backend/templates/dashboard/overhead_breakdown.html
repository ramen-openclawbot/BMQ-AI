Revenue-Based Allocation

Overhead is allocated based on each product's selling price relative to total revenue.

Formula:
Product Overhead = Total Overhead × (Product Revenue / Total Revenue)
This method ensures high-value products bear higher overhead costs, improving profitability analysis.
About Overhead Allocation

Overhead costs are fixed or semi-fixed costs that cannot be directly attributed to a specific product.

Common Categories:
  • Rent and utilities
  • Equipment depreciation
  • Maintenance and repairs
  • Administrative staff
  • Insurance
  • Office supplies

Accurate overhead allocation helps determine true product profitability.
