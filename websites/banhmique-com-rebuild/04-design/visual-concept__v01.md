## Premium F&B Visual Elements

### Photography Guidelines
- **Natural lighting**: Soft daylight or warm artificial light
- **Fresh ingredients**: Visible herbs, vegetables, clean preparation
- **Steam/warmth**: Subtle indication of fresh, hot food
- **Clean backgrounds**: White, light wood, or green accents
- **High contrast**: Vibrant but natural colors

### Typography Hierarchy
```
Headlines: Playfair Display, bold, 3rem desktop / 2rem mobile
Subheads: Inter, medium, 1.25rem
Body: Inter, regular, 1rem  
Buttons: Inter, semibold, 1rem
Captions: Inter, regular, 0.875rem
```

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous white space for premium feel
- 8px grid system for consistent spacing
- Rounded corners (16-24px) for friendly, modern look
- Subtle shadows for depth without heaviness
